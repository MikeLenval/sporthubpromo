Frequently Asked Questions

What makes SportHub different from other social networks?

SportHub is not just another social network - it's a specialized platform for sports fans that offers a unique "virtual stadium" concept. We integrate personalized fan profiles with official event profiles, creating a deeper and more personalized experience that distinguishes us from general social networks and even sports applications.

How does the "virtual stadium" feature work?

Our virtual stadium transforms team and event channels into virtual stands where fans can communicate, react to what's happening, and share emotions in real-time. It includes live chats, instant reactions to match events, integration with live broadcasts, and social interactions that create the feeling of being at a real stadium with other fans.

Will SportHub offer exclusive content?

Yes! SportHub partners with sports organizations to provide exclusive content only available on our platform. This includes behind-the-scenes materials, interviews, announcements, detailed match statistics, tactical analysis, expert predictions, and historical data. User-generated content and our community of like-minded fans also create unique value.

How will SportHub handle seasonal fluctuations in sports?

SportHub unites fans of all sports, which smooths out seasonal fluctuations - when one season ends, another begins. Additionally, our sports identity archiving feature maintains engagement year-round, regardless of event schedules. Our achievement system and social interactions further stimulate activity during off-seasons.

Will there be mobile apps available?

Yes, SportHub will offer native mobile applications for iOS and Android that provide access to all platform features. The apps will be optimized for mobile use with an adaptive interface, push notifications for match starts and key events, offline functionality, and integration with native device features.

About Us

SportHub is the world's fastest-growing sports fan network, uniting fans of all sports around the globe. Our platform is focused on the "second screen" culture and active fan community, offering personalized fan profiles and exclusive content from official partners.

Our key feature is the "virtual stadium" concept, where team and event channels on game day bring fans together for real-time communication. The project is developed using cutting-edge web technologies and aims to create a convenient and effective ecosystem for sports fans.

SportHub is your ultimate source for real-time sports scores and updates! We provide coverage of over 500 football leagues, cups, and tournaments worldwide. Stay updated with live match results, in-depth statistics, standings, top highlights, and live broadcasts.

Our system ensures automatic minute-by-minute updates on all football events, including scores, halftime and full-time results, goals, assists, cards, substitutions, and match stats. You can also catch key highlights from the world's biggest leagues, such as:

  • Spain – La Liga & Copa del Rey
  • Italy – Serie A & Coppa Italia
  • Germany – Bundesliga & DFB-Pokal
  • France – Ligue 1, UEFA Champions League & Europa League
  • International Tournaments – FIFA World Cup & UEFA European Championship

SportHub gives you access to detailed team data, including their last 10 matches, current standings, and real-time winning probabilities. Add your favorite teams and matches to your favorites for quick access to important updates!
